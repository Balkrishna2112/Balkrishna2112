About Me

Hello! I'm an AI and Business Coach with a rich background as a dedicated pharmacist. My journey in pharmacy has spanned many years, providing me with an invaluable foundation in health, precision, and patient care. But my curiosity doesn't stop there! I'm absolutely *crazy about Data Science* and its power to uncover insights and drive progress. This passion, combined with my extensive experience in the pharmaceutical world, allows me to offer a unique perspective on navigating complex business landscapes. I'm a firm believer in staying ahead of the curve, which is why I’m constantly exploring the *latest technologies and their impact on business. My coaching style is dynamic and forward-thinking, inspired by the boundless potential I see in artificial intelligence. Outside of my professional pursuits, my soul finds solace and exhilaration in the great outdoors. You can often find me planning my next **jungle safari, scaling mountains, or embarking on challenging treks*. These adventures mirror my approach to problem-solving – embracing the unknown and pushing boundaries. To maintain my focus and clarity, I am deeply committed to *yoga and meditation. And for a touch of escapism and inspiration, I thoroughly enjoy **watching science fiction movies*, which often spark creative ideas about the future we are building.
